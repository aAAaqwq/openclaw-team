## 2.2 构图专业术语

### 镜头类型

| 术语 | 缩写 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| Extreme Wide Shot | EWS | 极远景，展示全貌 |
| Wide Shot | WS | 远景，环境为主 |
| Full Shot | FS | 全身 |
| Medium Close-Up | MCU | 中特写，胸部以上 |
| Close-Up | CU | 特写，脸部 |
| Extreme Close-Up | ECU | 大特写，眼睛 |
| Over the Shoulder | OTS | 过肩镜头 |

### 构图法则

| 法则 | 英文 | 说明 |
|------|------|------|
| 三分法 | Rule of Thirds | 将画面分成9宫格 |
| 对称构图 | Symmetry | 镜像平衡 |
| 对角线 | Diagonal | 动态张力 |
| 引导线 | Leading Lines | 引导视线 |
| 框架 | Framing | 门窗等自然框 |
| 留白 | Negative Space | 简洁呼吸感 |
| 层次 | Depth/Layers | 前景中景远景 |

### 视角

## 2.4 风格+构图+光影组合示例

### 示例1：电影感人像

```
fashion portrait, young woman, studio lighting, rim light, dark background, dramatic shadows, high contrast, cinematic color grading, 35mm film look, Vogue magazine style --ar 3:4 --v 6
```

### 示例2：风景大片

```
epic mountain landscape, golden hour, dramatic clouds, volumetric lighting, long exposure, reflections in lake, deep blues and golds, Ansel Adams style, national geographic --ar 16:9 --v 6
```

### 示例3：产品展示

```
minimalist product photography, perfume bottle on marble, soft diffused lighting, subtle shadows, clean white background, luxury aesthetic, high-end beauty brand, 8k commercial photography --ar 1:1 --v 6
```

### 示例4：创意合成

```
surrealist art, floating islands, waterfall, dreamlike atmosphere, golden hour sky, volumetric clouds, matte painting, Epic Games concept art style --ar 16:9 --v 6
```
