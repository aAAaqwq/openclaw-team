# Content Language Strategy: English First

**Decided**: 2026-03-22
**Source**: [@Jiaxi_Cui tweet](https://x.com/Jiaxi_Cui/status/2035415033149264083)

---

## Decision

**Use English for X and LinkedIn. Do not build a Chinese X account.**

This resolves the long-running struggle over language choice.

---

## Reasoning (from @Jiaxi_Cui / Panda)

Chinese X is drifting toward an early Weibo atmosphere — flooded with marketing accounts. Too much noise, diminishing signal.

English X and LinkedIn have better conversion and are worth sustained effort.

XHS is different — high community quality, very vertical. But treat it separately:
- **Personal brand account** (high quality, curated): worth building
- **Customer acquisition / promotional accounts**: different strategy entirely

---

## Platform Strategy Summary

| Platform | Language | Strategy |
|----------|----------|---------|
| X / Twitter | **English** | Main personal brand channel |
| LinkedIn | **English** | Professional + longer-form |
| XHS (小红书) | Chinese | Separate vertical communities — high quality personal account |
| YouTube | TBD | Likely English-first |

---

## Implications for Redbook CLI

The `redbook` tool is still valuable for:
- XHS content creation and posting
- XHS community research and engagement
- Monitoring Chinese-language conversations about products/topics

XHS is a distinct channel with different rules — not "Chinese version of X" but its own high-quality vertical community platform.
